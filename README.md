# 🌐 **Project Name: Credora**

> *AI-native credit without collateral. Trust through intelligence, not assets.*

---

## 🚀 TL;DR

**Credora** is a decentralized, AI-powered credit protocol that enables undercollateralized lending for real-world borrowers. Rather than relying on crypto collateral, Credora leverages **AI-driven risk assessment**, **on-chain reputation**, and **Web3-native identity** to enable a new trust layer for finance — one where access is based on **credibility**, not capital.

---

## 🧠 The Problem

* DeFi has largely failed to serve **real-world borrowers**.
* Most protocols require **overcollateralization**, locking out small businesses, freelancers, and underbanked users globally.
* Meanwhile, off-chain lending is opaque, exclusive, and geographically limited.
* What’s missing? A **credible, decentralized way to assess trust** — without needing on-chain collateral.

---

## 🌍 The Vision

Build the future of credit access by:

* Replacing collateral with **intelligent underwriting**.
* Using **AI to evaluate borrower trustworthiness** via contextual, behavioral, and financial signals.
* Creating a **portable, composable on-chain credit identity** that grows with you.
* Turning reputation into the new collateral.

## 🔗 How Credora Works (End-to-End)

### 1. 🧬 Borrower Identity & Onboarding

* Connect wallet (EVM compatible)
* Optional: verify GitHub, LinkedIn, ENS, World ID, etc.
* Fill out a simple loan request form:

  * Purpose (e.g., inventory, freelance float)
  * Amount & duration
  * Real-world business signals (invoices, revenue proof, etc.)

### 2. 🧠 AI Underwriting Engine (Credora AI)

* LLM processes borrower data and generates:

  * **Credora Score™** (risk-based score)
  * **Loan terms** (amount, rate, risk tier)
  * **Explanations** for transparency (optional, for lenders)
* AI factors in:

  * Language tone & consistency
  * Source credibility
  * Business logic (cashflow, viability)
  * On-chain behavior (wallet age, tx history)
  * Web2 metadata (LinkedIn, site age, GitHub, etc.)

### 3. 📜 Loan Creation & Marketplace

* Loans that pass underwriting get listed
* Lenders see:

  * Borrower profile + AI-assigned tier
  * Repayment history (on-chain)
  * Optional: social signals or vouches (Web of Trust)
* Lenders choose to fund — loans are filled in tranches or pools

### 4. 💸 Disbursement & Repayment

* Borrower receives stablecoins (e.g. USDC)
* Repays over time (e.g. monthly/weekly)
* Smart contract enforces schedule and penalties
* Missed payments = risk score drop, public record

### 5. 🔁 Reputation Loop

* Credora issues **Credit Passport NFTs (Soulbound)**:

  * Records repayment history
  * Evolves with borrower (levels up)
  * Portable across DeFi (like a decentralized FICO)
* Future loans use this data for better rates
